stratus

MUSIC DISCOVERY AND CUSTOM RADIO

The stratus app provides users a simple seach interface to find music via the soundcloud API. Users can enter a song name, an artist, a genre of music, or even a random word to start.

Then, start a custom radio station based on that song selection or save the individual song to their personal playlist. Enjoy. Repeat.
